SEEC is known for taking projects from the drawing board to operational status. We are experts in building complicated systems from zero to completion. Some of our past projects are:

  • Supply, installation, testing and commissioning of 132/22 KV substation with 50 MVA transformer
  • Supply, installation, testing and commissioning of 132/22 KV substation with 25 MVA transformer
  • Installation, testing and commissioning of 132/22 KV substation with 12.5 MVA transformer
  • Installation testing and commissioning of MCC/PCC and control panels
  • Installation testing and commissioning of 11/22/33 KV Breaker panels
  • Installation testing and commissioning of 1 MW to 20 MW Co-generation plants with its auxiliary system at various sites
  • Installation testing and commissioning of 22 KV Breaker panel

  • Electrical equipment testing including - Transformers, CT, PT, LA, breakers, cables, earthing system, bus bars, generators, etc.
